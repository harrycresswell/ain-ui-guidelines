--- notes: | Alerts can be used when you need to give more information to the user based on their action or before they take an action. Use `.Alert` followed by the specific alert modifier. You can use `Alert--info`, `Alert--warning`, `Alert--error`, or `Alert--success`. If you need to show an alert on a themed background then use the `Alert--ghost` modifier. ---
